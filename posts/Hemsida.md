# Making a website

This website has been in the making since 2022. Back then I - like I do still - wanted a place where I could upload text online. The internet can be a good accountability partner.



Having some knowledge of coding since I was a child I began making a website. Not one of these template blogs I either would dislike or have to pay for to actually like - but my own. As time flew by interest stagnated and dropped, and I found other outlets for expressing myself online. Making the website wasn't what I wanted really - what I wanted was to write. And if I want to write I shouldn't bother if the website isn't looking exactly how I want it. And also, it doesn't equire coding. Very convinient.



A few times I came back with trying to finish what I started, but never ever reaching so far. Now four years later a culmination of factors have led me to take on this challenge once again. This time, I managed to pull it off.



One factor includes the discovery of an AI service. But not in the way you think. I work as a bike messenger, and one thing our company does is to lend out our labor for anyone willing to pay. And a sucessful AI startup that doesn't know what to do with all their money spends it on us. So after having worked in their office moving around tables and what not, I thought I might give their AI a try. I went to their site and asked it to make a website after my wishes. A few promts later, and some results I wasn't very happy with, I reached the monthly limit. That became a spark. I thought, I can do this better myself. And so I began. And here we are, a few weeks later, and I'm happy with how it turned out.



Now that this step is over, I can once again focus on what I really want. Writing.
