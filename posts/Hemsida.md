# Making a website

This website has been in the making since 2022. Back then I - like I do still - wanted a place where I could upload text online. The internet can be a good accountability partner.



I knew basic HTML and CSS from having experimented with coding from a young age, so I wanted to attempt to make my website all on my own - not using any template or website building program that would make something I either would dislike or have to pay for to actually like. However, coding takes time and as time flew by interest eventually declined, and I found other places to upload my text. Making the website wasn't what I really wanted - what I wanted was to write. Instead I found other places for that.



A few times I came back with trying to finish my website, but I never managed to. Until now, four years later.



One contributing factor is my discovery of an AI service. But not in the way you think. I work as a bike messenger, and one thing our company does is to lend out our labor for anyone willing to pay. And a sucessful AI startup that doesn't know what to do with all their money spends it on us. So after having worked in their office moving around tables and what not, I thought I might give their AI a try. I went to their site and asked it to make a website after my wishes. A few promts later, and some results I wasn't very happy with, I reached the monthly limit. That became a spark; I thought, I can do this better myself. And so I began, again. And here we are, a few weeks later, and I'm happy with how it turned out.



Now that this step is over, I can once again focus on what I really want to do. Write.
